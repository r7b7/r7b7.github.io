Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is R7B7 a consulting company?
A: No, R7B7 is not a consulting company. We do not take on paid consulting work. Our focus is entirely on research and software development.
Q: Can we hire R7B7 for technical consulting or software services?
A: We do not offer paid consulting or implementation services. However, we are happy to guide non-profit organizations and charities facing IT challenges, especially in areas related to scalability, performance, and system design.
Q: We’re a charity/non-profit—can we reach out for tech advice?
A: Absolutely. If you're a registered charity or nonprofit tackling technology challenges, feel free to contact us. While we don’t provide hands-on implementation, we’re happy to offer guidance where we can.
Q: Does R7B7 offer speaking engagements or workshops?
A: Yes, we’re open to participating in events, especially those that aim to encourage young girls and women to pursue careers in technology. If you're organizing a relevant event or workshop, please get in touch.
Q: What does R7B7 focus on?
A: R7B7 is a pure research and development company. We focus on deep, independent research around issues faced by companies running large-scale technology systems. We also build open-source tools that reflect the findings of our research.
